Charles R. Carignan

I am a graduate student in the Igic Lab at the University of Illinois at Chicago.
My research interests include plant population genetics, self-incompatibility, aquatic and terrestrial plant ecology
, and genetic mechanisms that regulate plant adaptations to changes in environmental conditions.
I currently am a TA for the Mendelian and Molecular Genetics course at UIC.

I received my B.Sc. from the School of Freshwater Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee where
I studied freshwater ecology with a focus on phytoplankton physiology.
