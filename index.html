I am a graduate student in the Igic Lab at the University of Illinois at Chicago.

My research interests include plant population genetics, self-incompatibility, aquatic and terrestrial plant ecology, and genetic mechanisms of plant adaptations to changes in water availability.

I received my B.Sc. from the School of Freshwater Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee where I studied Great Lakes ecology and phytoplankton physiology.
